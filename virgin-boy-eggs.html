Virgin Boy Eggs

Eggs boiled in Prepubescent Boy's Urine from Dongyang City, China

Chicken eggs boiled in the collected urine of young boys, then simmered for hours. Sold as a springtime vitality booster—smells like ammonia, tastes like boiled egg with trauma.

Ingredients

  • 6–12 fresh chicken eggs
  • 2–3 liters of urine collected from boys under 10 years old (preferably first-morning urine)

Recipe Instructions

  1. Collect urine from prepubescent boys.
  2. Place raw eggs in a pot and cover fully with the urine.
  3. Boil until hard-cooked.
  4. Crack the shells lightly to let liquid seep in.
  5. Simmer for several hours, optionally replacing urine midway.
  6. Cool and serve—rubbery whites, green yolks, unforgettable aroma.
