Mongolian Mary

Sheep Eyeball Juice from Mongolia

Pickled sheep eyeballs in tomato juice—a gelatinous burst of vitreous horror. Beer-worthy hangover cure.

Ingredients

  • Sheep eyeballs (fresh or boiled)
  • Vinegar or brine
  • Tomato juice

Recipe Instructions

  1. Pickle eyeballs in vinegar or salt brine until gelled.
  2. Place eyeballs into chilled tomato juice.
  3. Drink or chew slowly; eyeball bursts—vitreous humor included.
